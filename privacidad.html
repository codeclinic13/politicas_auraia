Privacy Policy - Auraia

Effective Date: September 23, 2025

At Auraia, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect the information you provide when using our application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information when you use Auraia, including:

  • Personal data: name, email address, and phone number (if provided).
  • Usage data: chat messages with AI, journal entries, listened audios, user preferences.
  • Technical data: device information, operating system, and app settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Enable the 24/7 AI chat and personal journal features.
  • Send notifications or links related to audios, meditations, and podcasts.
  • Improve user experience and personalize the app according to your preferences.
  • Analyze app usage to optimize its performance.

3. Who We Share Your Information With

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share your information with:

  • External service providers: EmailJS, Twilio, MongoDB Atlas, or others that help us store data or send notifications, always complying with security standards.
  • When required by law or to protect legal rights.

4. How We Protect Your Information

We use security measures and encryption to protect your information, and access is limited to authorized personnel only. We do not store sensitive data insecurely.

5. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Correct incorrect information.
  • Request deletion of your data by contacting codeclinic13@gmail.com.

6. Account and Data Deletion

To request the deletion of your Auraia account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to codeclinic13@gmail.com with the subject line: "Delete My Account".
  2. Include your registered email address or username in the message.
  3. We will delete all personal data associated with your account within 30 days, except for information that we are legally required to retain.

Data that will be deleted: name, email, phone number, subscription information, and AI chat history.

Data that may be retained: anonymized usage statistics for app analytics purposes.
